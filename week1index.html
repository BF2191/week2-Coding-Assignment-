Camping Trip

A camping trip can an amazing experience that will leave you with great memories. Camping is more than just a tent, its a chance to spend time with loved ones, or get in touch with nature. When people go camping its usually to travel and to unwind from all of lifes stressors.Planning a camping trip can be an eye opener for many, on what they are missing out on. The locations there are and views will blow you away.

How to plan a camping trip

  1. Decide where to go
  2. Aquire gear and equipment you will need to stay a few days
  3. Pack clothes fitting for the weather
  4. Pack food and water enough for everyone going and length of stay

What to do while camping

The choice is really up to each individual. Thats the beauty of it. Most people go to just get away from their normal life. To get away from cell phones, internet, or people. Others go for the chance to see new places and sights. Activities such as hunting, fishing, kayaking, or riding an offroad vehicle are some of the more popular choices. Camping is a hobby that can be done and enjoyed in so many different ways.

Activities to do while camping

  • hiking
  • fishing or hunting
  • relaxing
  • games like cornhole, board games, or cards
  • off road vehicle riding
  • swimming
  • enjoy undivided time with loved ones

Different ways to camp

  • Tent- probably the most common
  • Travel trailer- the most convient, with storage, a kitchen, and bathroom
  • Motorhome- all in one vehicle with and on board bathroom
  • Cabin-not tradional but most cabins are secluded by capming areas

    The beautiful thing is there is no right and wrong way. It comes down to personal preference.
